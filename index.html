What We Stand For

Our Platform

⚙️

Discord Server Reform

We will restructure the server from the ground up — clearer channels, better organization, and transparent moderation. Every member deserves a space that functions with fairness and clarity.

⚖️

Economic Equality

No member should be left behind. Our JSP senators will push for fair distribution of in-server resources, equal opportunity in RP economies, and accountability for those who exploit the system.

🎫

Ticket System Overhaul

The current ticket system is broken. We will modernize it faster response times, clearer categories, and a fair appeals process so no grievance goes unheard or unresolved.

🏛️

New Institutions & Agencies

Strong roleplay needs strong institutions. We will champion the creation of new RP agencies, regulatory bodies, and oversight committees to enrich the simulation and give members more ways to engage.

Stand With the JSP

Together, Aurelius, Shariah, Scott, Sheepy sleep, Hibiscus, and Ishmael will bring justice to the system and to every member.
